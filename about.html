About the Sportan Project

Sportan was founded on a simple premise: technology should bring us together in the real world, not keep us isolated behind screens. We noticed that while there are apps for everything else, finding a simple pick-up game of soccer or basketball in a new city was still surprisingly difficult.

Based on our experience in digital infrastructure and community building, we developed Sportan to be a fast, lightweight solution for athletes of all skill levels. Our platform allows users to find nearby courts, see who is currently playing, and join games with a single tap.

Our Technical Vision

We leverage advanced web automation and real-time data to map sports facilities across the globe. By focusing on performance and user experience, we ensure that you spend less time on your phone and more time on the field.
